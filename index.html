📖 How to Play

🧩

Unscramble the Sentence

You'll see a jumbled sentence. Tap the words in the correct order to reconstruct the original sentence.

👆

Tap to Build

Tap a word to add it to your answer. Tap a placed word to remove it. Arrange all words correctly, then hit Submit.

⏱️

Beat the Clock

You have 60 seconds per game. Sentences get longer as you score more. Speed is key!

❤️

Lives System

You start with 3 lives. Each wrong submission costs 1 life. Game ends at 0 lives or when time runs out.

⭐

Scoring

+1 point for each correct sentence. Try to beat your high score! Difficulty increases with each correct answer.
