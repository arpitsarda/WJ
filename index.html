📖 How to Play

🧩

Unscramble the Sentence

You'll see a jumbled sentence. Tap the words in the correct order to reconstruct the original sentence.

👆

Drag to Rearrange

Tap a word to move it to your answer. You can drag and drop words within your answer to rearrange them!

⏱️

Beat the Clock & Combos

You have 3 minutes. Correct answers give bonus time. Answer quickly within 5s to build a Combo Multiplier for huge scores!

🔥

Hard Mode & Hints

In Hard Mode, extra "dummy" words are mixed in—ignore them! Stuck? Use the 💡 HINT button (you get 3 per game).

❤️

Lives System

You start with 3 lives. Each wrong submission costs 1 life. Game ends at 0 lives or when time runs out.
